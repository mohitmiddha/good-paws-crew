📚 Word Wizards: Vocabulary Quest 📚

Archmage Lexicon guards the Vocabulary Realm, where words have been scattered into scrambled letters. Tap the letter tiles in order to restore each word from its clue — across 11 realms covering geography, space, animals, sports, logic, maths, science, language arts, art, design, and Spanish.

Pick a nickname, choose your wordsmith, and start restoring the realm!

How to Play

📚
1. Pick any realmAll 11 realms are open from the start — geography with Compass, space with Astra, animals with Bramble, sports with Blaze, logic with Zin, maths with Numeria, science with Fizzle, language arts with Willow, art with Chroma, design with Cogg, and Spanish with Flores.
🔤
2. Read the clueEach round gives you a clue and a bank of scrambled letters. Tap letters in order to spell the word the clue describes.
↩️
3. Fix mistakesTap a filled slot to remove that letter and try again — no penalty for adjusting.
🔀
4. Rounds shuffle every timeEach realm has a bigger word pool than one round uses, so replaying pulls a fresh mix.
🏅
5. Earn badges & a certificateSpell more words correctly for more badges. Restore all 11 realms to unlock your Archmage certificate.
