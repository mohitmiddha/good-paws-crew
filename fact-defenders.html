How to Defend

🛡️
1. Pick any outpostAll 11 outposts are open from the start — geography with Terra, space with Nova, animals with Fang, sports with Blitz, logic with Cipher, maths with Vector, science with Volt, language arts with Lex, art with Prism, design with Forge, and Spanish with Sol.
👾
2. Read the claimEach wave sends 8 creatures, one at a time, each carrying a claim. Decide if it's TRUE or FALSE before it reaches the wall.
⏱️
3. Beat the danger barThe bar fills as the creature marches closer. Tap the correct gate before it reaches the end!
🔀
4. Waves shuffle every timeEach outpost has a bigger pool of claims than one wave uses, so replaying pulls a fresh mix.
📛
5. Earn badges & a certificateMore correct gates means more badges. Defend all 11 outposts to unlock your Alliance certificate.
