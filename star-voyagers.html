The story so far…

Deep in the galaxy, an ancient AI called the Archivist scattered the Great Archive of knowledge across 11 star systems during a cosmic storm. Commander Zeta has recruited your crew aboard the starship Nova Wanderer for one mission: fly the star map, unlock each system, and recover the Archive — earning your rank as an Official Star Voyager.

How to play 🚀

🗺️
1. Fly the star mapYour 11 star systems are laid out along a route. Tap the glowing (unlocked) system to travel there.
❓
2. Answer a 5-question challengeEach system gives you 5 questions, 20 seconds each. Answer well to earn 1-3 energy cells (stars) and unlock the next system.
🔀
3. Questions shuffle every timeReplay a completed system anytime for a fresh mix of questions and a chance at a better score.
⚡
4. Chain a Boost streakAnswer several in a row correctly for bonus points.
🕹️
5. Visit the Holo-ArcadeFree-play bonus mini-games are always open from the star map — no challenge needed. A perfect 5/5 also unlocks a surprise bonus round.
🛰️
6. Earn your certificateUnlock and earn a badge in all 11 systems to claim your Official Star Voyager certificate.
