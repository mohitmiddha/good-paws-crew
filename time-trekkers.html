⏳ Time Trekkers: History Quest ⏳

A mysterious Time Fog has scrambled history across 11 ancient eras — the order of events, steps, and facts has come completely undone. Chrono-Keeper Vex has recruited you to fix the timeline by putting each era's scrambled sequence back in the right order, covering geography, space, animals, sports, logic, maths, science, language arts, art, design, and Spanish.

Pick a nickname, choose your trekker, and start repairing history!

Choose your Trekker

How to Trek

⏳
1. Pick any eraAll 11 eras are open from the start — geography with Atlas, space with Celeste, animals with Fossil, sports with Olympia, logic with Riddle, maths with Euclid, science with Dr. Sparks, language arts with Quill, art with Muse, design with Gearhart, and Spanish with Isabela.
🧩
2. Unscramble the sequenceEach era gives you 4 scrambled items. Drag them (or use the ▲▼ buttons) into the correct order, then tap Check Order.
🔀
3. Puzzles shuffle every timeEach era has 3 different sequencing puzzles — replay for a fresh one and to earn better relics.
📜
4. Earn relics & a certificateSolve it in fewer tries for more relics (up to 3). Repair all 11 eras to unlock your Time Trekker certificate.
💎
5. Collect gemsEvery solved era earns gems, with bonus gems for a first-try solve and for finishing every era.
