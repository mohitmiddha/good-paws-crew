🧳 Passport Quest: Postcards from Everywhere 🧳

Dame Adventuria has scattered a deck of postcard pairs across 11 famous stops around the globe — geography, space, animals, sports, logic, maths, science, language arts, art, design, and Spanish. Flip cards two at a time to match each postcard with its fact, fill your passport with stamps, and become an Official Globetrotter.

Choose your Globetrotter

How to Play

🧳
1. Pick any stopAll 11 stops are open from the start — geography with Compass, space with Skye, animals with Safari, sports with Rio, logic with Sphinx, maths with Pyramid, science with Reef, language arts with Story, art with Palette, design with Brix, and Spanish with Flor.
🃏
2. Flip and matchEach stop has 12 cards (6 pairs). Flip two cards at a time — if they match, they stay face-up with a fun fact. If not, they flip back.
⭐
3. Fewer flips = more stampsMatch all 6 pairs in as few tries as you can for up to 3 passport stamps.
🔀
4. Boards shuffle every timeReplay a stop for a freshly shuffled layout and a shot at a better score.
🎫
5. Earn a certificateCollect all 11 passport stamps to unlock your Official Globetrotter certificate.
