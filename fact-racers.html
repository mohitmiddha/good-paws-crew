How to Race

🏁
1. Pick any circuitAll 11 circuits are open from the start — geography with Terra, space with Astro, animals with Cheetah, sports with Turbo, logic with Riddle, maths with Digit, science with Quantum, language arts with Story, art with Palette, design with Gear, and Spanish with Veloz.
🚦
2. Pick the correct laneEach race asks 10 quick two-option questions. Tap the option you think is correct to send your car speeding forward.
🏎️
3. Watch your car moveCorrect picks boost your car toward the checkered flag. Wrong picks still let you race on, just without the boost.
🔀
4. Circuits shuffle every timeEach circuit has a full pool of questions, so replaying can pull a fresh mix.
🏆
5. Earn badges & a certificateMore correct picks means more trophy stars. Finish all 11 circuits to unlock your Grand Circuit certificate.
